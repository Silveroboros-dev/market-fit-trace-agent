# Rejected Markets Notes

Record why tempting markets are wrong. This is the most important annotation artifact after the gold label.

Template:

```text
## eval_001

- rejected_market_id:
  - tempting because:
  - rejected because:
  - label impact:
```

Rules:

- A title match is not enough.
- Wrong horizon usually downgrades direct to indirect or weak.
- Wrong resolution source can make a direct-looking market risky.
- A noisy asset-price or index market is usually a weak proxy for a specific thesis.
